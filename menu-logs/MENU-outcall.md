# RECIPE
##### GIN SOUR
* 60ml gin
* 22.5ml lemon juice
* 22.5ml symple syrup

Shake 20-25 sec, double strain.  
Garnish with lemon peel.

##### RUM MILK PUNCH
* 45ml spiced rum
* 90ml milk
* 22.5ml simple syrup
* 1 dash vanilla extract

Garnish with cinnamon and nutmeg.
